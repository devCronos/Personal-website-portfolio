Welcome to my personal sandbox


I'm a passionate web developer who has been a pharmacist in a past life.


I make a huge deal out of making my interfaces first and foremost human friendly and then aesthetically pleasing. After all, there is a human behind each and every internet machine and sometimes, their steps must be guided through your graphic architecture, their clicks must be rewarded with just the right hover effect or animation and their attention directed towards the main purpose of the website/application. I will not let my finger touch the keyboard until I fully empathize and understand the future user and grasp the big picture of the project.


I found myself browsing daily, looking for standards-based methodology and learning from the best to ultimately make my work more understandable for the user as well as for my teammates. I can also write SEO friendly code.


I will always appreciate critique, tips, advices on my craft as well as on how to do better in the industry.
